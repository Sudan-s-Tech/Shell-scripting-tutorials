Commands 
-------------------
1.clear - to clear the terminal 
2.who am i - to check the username , terminal  id and time and date of login
3.pwd - present directory in which shell is in
---------

VIEW SYSTEM CALENDAR , DATE
==================================


cal - to show calendar 
 ---
cal 7 2023 - show calendar of 7th month 
cal feb 2023 - show calendar of feb month 
------------

date - show day month date time and timezone and year
-------
date '+DATE:%m-%y%nTIME:%H:%M:%S'
-------
